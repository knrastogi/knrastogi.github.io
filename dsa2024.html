Course Objective

This course aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of various data structures and algorithms, their implementation, and application in solving complex computational problems. Students will gain practical skills in designing and analyzing algorithms and understanding their efficiency and limitations.

Syllabus

  • Introduction to Data Structures and Algorithm
  • Algorithm Complexity and Big-O Notation
  • Arrays, Linked Lists, Stacks, and Queues
  • Trees, Graphs, and Heaps
  • Searching, Sorting and Algorithms and Hashing
